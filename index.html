Problem Statement & Technical Challenges

Business Context

  • E-commerce platforms need real-time price optimization
  • Dynamic pricing increases revenue by 2-5% on average
  • Traditional rule-based systems lack adaptability
  • Need to balance profit margins with competitive positioning

Technical Challenges

  • High-frequency decision making (sub-second responses)
  • Multi-dimensional state space (price, demand, competition)
  • Scalability across millions of products
  • Model interpretability for business stakeholders
  • Cold start problem for new products

Our Technical Approach

We'll implement a hybrid ML system combining reinforcement learning (DQN) for price optimization, LSTM networks for demand forecasting, and XGBoost for price elasticity analysis, all orchestrated through a microservices architecture.

DQN Approach for Dynamic Pricing

State Representation

Current price, demand forecast, competitor prices, inventory level, seasonality features

Action Space

Price adjustments: [-20%, -10%, -5%, 0%, +5%, +10%, +20%]

Reward Function

R = profit_margin × demand_volume - inventory_cost - competitive_penalty
